Retool


Built with React on the client-side, Django on the server-side, and using a PostgreSQL database. Retool is a web application that allows its users to track and visualize their vehicle maintenance services, their prices, spent money and other data. It also includes a reminders function.

UNvest


Built with SpringBoot, ASP.NET, Flask, Nest and Express. UNvest is an investment web platform that provides users with a wide range of features, including buying and selling stocks, tracking their investment portfolio, and much more. In addition to these features, UNvest provides users with relevant news about stocks, allowing them to make informed decisions and increase their confidence in their transactions.

Unplanned


Built with NextJS, React, Tailwind CSS, PostgreSQL, NextAuth, Google Places API and Vercel. A random place recommendation-based application. Users can select categories of places they are interested in (Restaurants, Museums, Galleries, Theaters) and filter by tags or conditions (budget, distance, or area). After the selection, the system performs a weighted random selection (with weighting values adjusted according to the user's profile) and displays a recommended place for the user.
